  • Security Information

    This service requires your UQ username and password as it simulates a web-browsing session to the SOM MBBS Timetable page to get your timetable data. Ultimately, you have to trust that I am honest, non-malicious, and handle your password correctly.

    Note, however, that I use this site myself, so I do have vested interest in maintaining good security (plus, I also used to work as a sysadmin, so there's some professional pride too).

  • More information

    Some technical guarantees:

    • All data (including this page, your username, password and timetable) are sent encrypted (SSL).
    • I will never store your password in any format that is reversible or recoverable. I use a uniquely-salted hash (SHA-1).
