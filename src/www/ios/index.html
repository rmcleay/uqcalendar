  • What is this?

    This software will instead add your UQ timetable to iCal. It will:

    • Automatically update from SiNet.
    • Display lecture titles correctly.
    • Show your timetable for the current semester.
  • Why?

    UQ has provided us with a timetable that is fairly unusable. Although they provide an iCal file, we need to manually download it, and edit it to make lecture titles display correctly. This combined with UQ's propensity to change the timetable without much notice (for medicine at least) causes problems.
